📋 General Instructions

  1. This test contains multiple choice questions. Each question has four options (A, B, C, D).
  2. Select the most appropriate answer by clicking on the option.
  3. You can navigate between questions using the question palette on the right side.
  4. Questions can be marked for review and answered later.
  5. The test will automatically submit when the time limit is reached.
  6. You can submit the test manually at any time before the time limit.
  7. Once submitted, you cannot make any changes to your answers.
  8. Use the fullscreen mode for better focus and to avoid distractions.
  9. Ensure stable internet connection throughout the test.
  10. Do not refresh the page or use browser back/forward buttons during the test.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Negative marking applies as per the test pattern
  • Calculator and other external tools are not allowed
  • Any attempt to switch tabs or applications will be recorded
  • The test will auto-save your progress every 30 seconds
  • In case of technical issues, contact support immediately
